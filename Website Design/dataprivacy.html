Data Privacy Policies


What kind of information does this Survey collect?
Through the survey we gather information that serves the purpose of the project, as explained below :
• Basic Information : Home Country, Age group and course type
• Opinions : provided by adversary(you) on the given survey subject.

How information collected in this survey is used?
The information collected in this survey through the questionnaire is used to verify the level of data privacy, that can be ensured to a participant. The basic information like home country, age group and course type are only used for analytical purposes and the information is protected from misuse by the third parties.

The opinions provided by the users doesn't reveal the identity of the individual. The questionnaire is designed in a manner that avoids the risk of privacy invasion of an individual.

Do we use cookies?
No, the survey doesn't use any sort of cookies.

Do we disclose any information to outside/third parties?
No, the information collected through the survey is stored in the database. The database is protected by applying the differential privacy algorithms on it. This ensures that the data is not compromised. The project doesn't share the information with any other third/outside parties.

Transparency and choice
Everyone have different types and kinds of privacy concerns. The project goal is to just collect the opinions from the International students, apply the concept of Differential Privacy over that data and use the resulting data to analyze the problem situation.

Information you share
The present survey doesn't allow you to share any information. It allows you to participate in the survey and also to invite others to participate in the survey.

Accessing and updating Personal Information
This survey doesn't store any personal information from the survey participant, so there is no need of accessing and updating the Personal Information.

Information we share
The project doesn't share the information provided by the user directly to others. The aim of the project is to use the information provided by the survey participant and analyze it while protecting privacy of the data, i.e., “Privacy preserving Data Analysis”.
We share the overall analysis results of the survey but not the individual data. The main objective of this project is to prove that research studies can be conducted without compromising the privacy of the individuals participating in the survey.

When do these Privacy Policies take effect?
These policies aplly to a participant the moment they check the 'I accept all data privacy policies' and click on submit.Therefore, when you are taking part in the survey, your data is already protected by our privacy policies.

Changes to the Policy
When changes are made to this policy the participant is informed about the same.
